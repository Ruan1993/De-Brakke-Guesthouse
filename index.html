About De Brakke

A breezy haven designed for comfort and close proximity to the stunning Stilbaai coastline.

De Brakke Guesthouse offers a tranquil and stylish stay in the sought-after area of Stilbaai West, Western Cape. Our design philosophy centers on light, open spaces and a refreshing color palette that mirrors the nearby ocean, ensuring a serene and stress-free environment for all our guests.

Perfect for families or two couples traveling together, the guesthouse features two beautifully appointed bedrooms, each with its own luxury en-suite bathroom. Enjoy the convenience of a fully equipped open-plan living area and the peace of mind of secure, on-site parking.
